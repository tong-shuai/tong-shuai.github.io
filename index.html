    Tsinghua University

    • Beijing, China

    Shuai is currently a postdoc in Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Yunhao Liu and Prof. Jiliang Wang. He received his PhD degree from Tsinghua University in 2024 and B.S. degree from Nankai University in 2019. His research interests include LoRa, wireless communication and sensing, and building various innovative systems and applications in the area of Internet of Things.
